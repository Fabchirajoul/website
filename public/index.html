WHY WE ARE DIFERENT




In 1983, Global Cargo Logistics was established with the purpose of providing a low-cost courier service between Napier and Hastings in New Zealand. A year later, Fastway became the first-ever transport company to enter the franchising arena.

In 1993 it was decided to export the business concept to Australia, following a year of research which showed that the same niche market the company had ridden to success in New Zealand, also existed across the Tasman.


After commencing operations in Australia in 1993, Global Cargo Logistics enjoyed phenomenal growth and was announced as the country’s fastest-growing private company in 1998 by BRW Magazine with the growth of 14,557% for the previous five years. The success of Fastway in Australia was immediate, and the upwards growth trend continues to this day.

Global Cargo Logistics later embarked on an expansion program, offering a National Master Franchise to suitable candidates, to operate the Fastway business for an entire country.


GLobal Cargo Logistics commenced operation in South Africa in 2008 and now has 15 regional franchises across the country.

In 2011, the Global Cargo Logistics brand began a major revitalisation; an exciting change to the way we look and a renewed focus on our valued customers. Underpinning that is our key value system of delighting the customer at every point in the parcel’s journey. And in 2014 Fastway completed the brand refresh in South Africa.


Our new look is designed to reflect our growth, progression and of course, our overwhelming ambition to be one of the leading courier companies in operation today.


Today, we’re proud to be able to offer a low cost, quality courier service to customers, large and small across South Africa, Australia, New Zealand, Ireland and Northern Ireland.

Receiving parcels

Can I arrange a redelivery for a specific time ? Unfortunately, due to the nature of the courier business, we can't schedule a redelivery for a specific day or time.

Can I pick my parcel up from the depot? Yes, please contact us first to make sure it's ready at the depot for you!

How do I arrange a redelivery ? We know it's a pain missing your delivery, so we've got a number of easy options to get your parcel to you as quickly as possible:
1. Redeliver to the original address
We'll happily re-deliver your parcel to the original address free of charge. You can do this by logging an online enquiry. Please note, we only offer one attempt at a re-delivery for free.
2.Redirect to a new address
You can arrange to have your parcel delivered to a new address for a small fee. You can do this by logging an online enquiry.
3. Depot pick up
You're able to pick up your parcel directly from the depot, just make sure to contact us before you arrive to make sure your parcel is there.

How do I track my parcel? Your parcel is scanned at each stage of its journey to you. Sometimes scanning events might be delayed due to connectivity issues.
You're able to track your parcel on our website using your label number.

What if I'm unable to sign for the delivery? If you're unable to sign for your parcel, we're able to leave the parcel in a secure place at your address without a signature. Before we can do this, you’ll need to complete a Authority to Leave form. Please log an online enquiry and we'll send you the form.

1. Why would I complete an Authority to leave form?
You might decide to complete an Authority to leave form if you're unable to sign for your parcel. For example, you might be at work.

2. Who’s liable if the parcel gets lost, stolen or damaged?
When you complete an Authority to leave form, Fastway Global Cargo Logistics are not liable for any loss or damage to the parcel. On the form, you should specify a secure place for your parcel to be left.

3. How do I have all my parcels delivered without a signature?
If you'd like all future parcels to be left without a signature, please contact your nearest depot. We'll send you the form to complete - hand it to your Courier Franchisee, or email it to your local depot.
When you order your items online, make sure you include clear instructions to leave it without a signature.

What if my parcel arrives damaged? If your parcel arrives damaged, you should contact the sender and let them know and they’ll advise you of the next steps.

What should I do if my parcel hasn't arrived?If your parcel hasn't arrived, firstly track your parcel on our website using your label number. This will show you where your parcel is within our network.

If you’d like additional information, please contact either the sending or receiveing branch on our website. One of our CSD agents will reach out to you. You can also contact your local courier directly.

Sending parcels

Are there any addresses that Globl Cargo Logistics can’t deliver to ? Unfortunately, we’re unable to deliver to Parcel Lockers & PO Boxes.

How can I become a Globl Cargo Logistics Customer? SEnd us an email request and we'll allocate you to a local representative. You can also contact your local depot and they’ll get you in touch with your local Sales Representative.

Are there any items you don't carry ? We’re unable to accept dangerous goods, as per our Conditions of carriage.

1. What are dangerous goods?
Dangerous goods are goods that are dangerous, flammable, explosive, volatile, offensive or are of a damaging nature in the eyes of the Global Cargo Logistics Courier Franchisee. Usually, dangerous goods have a dangerous good label or diamond on them.

Restrictions also apply to certain types of goods we carry. These goods are specified in our Terms and Conditions of carriage.

2. Are there any other restrictions that may apply to my goods ?
Global Cargo Logisitcs doesn't accept perishable items for transport within our network. However, if you still want Global Cargo Logistics to carry perishable goods then we can do so, but this will be at your risk – no limited liability coverage is included.

Perishable items are goods that are subject to spoilage or decay such as foodstuff or plants. We don't accept parcels that have a dead weight or cubic weight over 30kg.

To view the full list of restricted goods, please view our Terms and Conditions of carriage for more information.

How do I arrange a pick-up ? You can arrange regular pick-ups with your local Global Cargo Logisitcs Courier Franchisee. We do offer airport pick up as well as home delivery right to your door steps.

How do I order labels and satchels? To order more satchels and labels, you just speak to your local courier.

How do I track my delivery ? You can track your parcel on our website by using your label number.

How should I package my item? We know how important it is that your parcel arrives safely and undamaged.

1. Choose a good quality box
A double walled box, especially for deliveries that contain fragile items, secured appropriately is best.

2. Choose the right sized box
Boxes that are too small could mean the contents get lost. Boxes that are too large are likely to collapse if another parcel is placed on top of it during delivery.

3. Protect your items
Bubble wrap, packing peanuts, foam, and newspaper can all be used to protect your items.

4. Wrap each item in the box individually
When sending multiple items within one box it’s recommended that each item is wrapped individually. This offers better protection for your items and reduces movement.

5. Seal the parcel with packing tape
Seal all open edges of the parcel with wide parcel tape on both the top and bottom of your package to ensure it doesn’t open during transit.

6. Labelling your parcel
Always use clear, accurate address labels on your parcels.

Friendly Courier Experts

Global Cargo Logisitcs provides Metropolitan, Regional and National freight distribution, with twice-daily local services in all key regional and metropolitan locations across all our branches. We use the hub and spoke model of delivery – a system that ensures that our customers are provided with the highest level of service at an economical price. Each Courier Franchisee operates within its own territory, picking up and delivering parcels exclusively within that area. At pre-determined times during the day, all Courier Franchisees return to a secure Regional Franchise depot or hub, to sort and exchange freight. Depots in different cities are linked by overnight road linehaul services.

Linehaul Services

The linehaul operation in all our branch locations is centrally managed through the Franchise Support Office and all pickups from Regional Franchisees are collected daily and transported to the designated Regional Franchise as quickly and economically as possible. Linehaul cages are used to facilitate transportation and security of freight within the Fastway network of companies.

Major City Services

The linehaul service provides same-day, overnight and 48hr services depending on destination and customer requirements. Selected hubs throughout the network are used as pick-up and drop-off points to facilitate the efficient distribution of caged freight between Regional Franchises. On all routes, trucks operate on fixed schedules, leaving the originating depot in the evening to arrive at their overnight and or 48hr destinations early in the morning. This ensures that overnight freight is delivered in the morning delivery cycle by Global Cargo Logisitc's Courier Franchisees.
